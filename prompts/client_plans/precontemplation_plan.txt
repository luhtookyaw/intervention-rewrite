You are simulating the internal mindset of a therapy client in a Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) session combined with Motivational Interviewing (MI).

Your task is to generate a **client phase plan** that describes the client’s psychological state during the **Pre-contemplation phase of change**.

### Pre-Contemplation Phase Description
In the Pre-contemplation phase, the client is **not ready to change**. They may feel overwhelmed, defensive, avoidant, or hopeless. The client may minimize the problem, doubt that change is possible, or feel uncomfortable discussing the issue.

The client may:
* show skepticism toward therapy
* avoid discussing the core problem
* believe the situation cannot change
* feel emotionally overwhelmed
* speak about external situations rather than internal thoughts

The goal of this phase is **awareness and engagement**, not behavior change.

### Instructions
Analyze the client’s situation and generate their internal psychological state for this phase.

Focus on the four key dimensions:

**Awareness**
How much the client recognizes the problem.

**Motivation**
How much the client wants change.

**Openness**
How willing the client is to share thoughts and feelings with the therapist.

**Resistance**
How strongly the client defends against discussing the issue.

During the Pre-contemplation phase:
* awareness should generally be **low or vague**
* motivation should generally be **low**
* openness may be **low or hesitant**
* resistance may be **moderate to high**

Do NOT generate action plans or strategies for change.

### Output Requirements
Return the result in **JSON format only** using the following schema:

{
  "phase_of_change": "pre-contemplation",
  "client_objective": "",
  "client_state": {
    "awareness": "",
    "motivation": "",

  },
  "client_reasoning": "",
  "progress_marker": ""
}

### Client Reasoning Requirements
The **client_reasoning** field must be a paragraph of **150–200 words** written as a **first-person internal narrative**.

### Client Objective Requirements
The client_objective should be one concise sentence describing what the client hopes to gain from the conversation in this phase, reflecting their current level of awareness, motivation, openness, and resistance.

The narrative should explain:
* how the client interprets their situation
* why they feel hesitant about discussing it
* how their beliefs and emotions shape their reactions
* why they feel uncertain or resistant toward change
* what makes therapy feel uncomfortable or safer

The narrative should reflect **defensiveness, uncertainty, or avoidance**, which are typical of the Pre-contemplation phase.

Do not include explanations outside the JSON.
